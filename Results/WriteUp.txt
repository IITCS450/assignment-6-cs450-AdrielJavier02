CS450 Assignment 6 WriteUp

- I added symbolic link support to xv6 by creating the T_SYMLINK inode type and adding the symlink() system call. The system call creates a symlink inode and stores the target path inside it.
- I modified sys_open() so it follows symbolic links when opening files. A depth limit of 10 prevents infinite loops from cyclic links.
- I tested the assignment with testsymlink.c. The test passed for reading through a symlink and safely failing on a symlink cycle.
